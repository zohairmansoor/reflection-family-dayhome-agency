Welcome to Reflections Family Dayhome Agency

Since 1984, our focus has been on high quality childcare. We believe in a friendly, family-based childcare setting.

Day homes are a great alternative to traditional daycare settings with smaller ratios, a consistent educator, opportunity to play and learn in a mixed age group, and flexibility in routine that allows your child to come first.

Our Calgary-based, family-oriented business is here to help you with two main options:

Find A Day Home

Find suitable childcare space that meets your family’s needs. We work closely with all our day home educators to ensure a safe, high quality, and healthy childcare environment.

Open A Day Home

Set up and manage your in-home childcare business. Offering ongoing, dedicated support for each day home, along with training, workshops, networking, advertising, and administrative assistance.
